# Notice

The legacy trainer integration follows the structure of an Ultralytics YOLOv8 training implementation and is therefore released under GPL-3.0-or-later.

The scientific component names and equations correspond to the manuscript **“DAQ-YOLO: A High Precision Counting Model for Maize Seedlings in Dense Scenes.”** Users should cite the final published article when bibliographic details become available.

This repository distinguishes between:

- equations explicitly stated in the manuscript;
- engineering choices needed to turn under-specified equations into executable code; and
- compatibility fallbacks used when a standard YOLO head does not output localization quality.
